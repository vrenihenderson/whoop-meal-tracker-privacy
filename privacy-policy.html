Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026  ·  Operated by Vreni Henderson

This Privacy Policy describes how the Whoop Meal Tracker application ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Whoop health data — With your explicit permission via Whoop's OAuth flow, we access data from your Whoop account including daily calorie burn, recovery scores, sleep data, workout data, and body measurements.
  • Meal data — Meal logs and nutritional information that you manually enter into the App.
  • Basic profile information — Your Whoop profile details (name, basic stats) used to personalise your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the App's features:

  • Displaying your daily calorie burn and activity data from Whoop
  • Tracking your meal intake and nutritional goals
  • Generating personalised meal suggestions based on your calories burned

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the above.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Whoop — Data is retrieved from Whoop's API in accordance with Whoop's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal requirements — If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally within the App and is not transmitted to any external servers beyond what is necessary to communicate with the Whoop API. We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the App. You may delete your data at any time by disconnecting your Whoop account within the App or by contacting us directly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to your Whoop account at any time via your Whoop account settings

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
